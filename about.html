We have been providing worldclass services to Nigerian consumers for over 7 years

Find out how

2012: The journey to democratize credit in Nigeria began...

One Credit was founded in Lagos, Nigeria. The company provided loans to salary earners only, using a paper based application process that required supporting documentation.

2015: More credit for more people...

With the introduction of the unique Bank Verification Number (BVN) and national credit bureau services, it becomes possible for us to provide more loans to more people, not just salary earners. The company broadened its service offering, leveraging a network of agents on the ground to get lending services in the hands of thousands of Nigerians under the OneFi brand.
