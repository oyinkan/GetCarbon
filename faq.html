About Carbon

How does Carbon work?

Carbon makes it easy to access amazing financial services, at the click of a button. You can get instant loans, make all your financial transactions, and invest money with high interest returns. Carbon is your trusted digital friend, always ready to help you out. Visit our website to learn more.

Is Carbon a bank? Where are you located?

Carbon is a service provided by One Finance & Investments Ltd (RC. 1044655), a Finance company licensed and regulated by the Central Bank of Nigeria. Even though we offer a service that is 100% online, you can still reach us whenever you need via our customer support channels or any of our social media.

Why is my Bank Verification Number (BVN) required to use Carbon?

We request your BVN and selfie as a security measure. This is used to verify that the individual using the Carbon service is the same as the owner of the provided bank account. This is to ensure that your details cannot be used without your authorization, in the event a 3rd party has access to your account details.
Please note: Your BVN does not provide access to your account. If in doubt, we encourage you to confirm this from your bank before you proceed.

What are the required documents or collateral for a Carbon loan?

Carbon loans do not require collateral or documents - our process is entirely paperless! All you need is an Android device, a data connection, a means of identification, as well as valid bank details.

I don’t have an Android device – can I access Carbon from a computer or from another operating system?

Yes! Carbon is currently available on Android devices via the Google Play Store and on our web portal here. We are working on getting Carbon out to even more platforms in the coming months, so stay tuned!

I don’t know my BVN. What do I do?

Just dial *565*0#.
Please note: This will only work if you are making the request from the same phone number currently linked to your bank account.

What is a client ID? How do I find mine?

Your client ID is the unique number Carbon uses to identify you. Think of it as your account number with us. It is displayed in your app, sent via SMS when your account is created, and contained in all account e-mails we send to you. It can also be found by logging into your app.
Please take note of this ID as it should be used for resolving account issues speedily or on any repayment transaction details for quick identification.

Can I update my banking / personal information?

You can update your banking details and all your personal information in the app.
If you have a running loan with us and need to update or change certain details, please use the ‘Your Profile’ app menu, or contact us via our dedicated support channels for assistance.

How do I check my account balance(s) or loan details?

Simply log in to your Carbon account (via the app or web) to view all details concerning your loan, investments and transactions.

Who should I contact to submit a comment, question or complaint?

You can drop us a message on customer@getcarbon.co and an agent will respond swiftly.
Feel free to reach out to us any time via our dedicated phone lines or find us on social media (Facebook, Twitter, Instagram and Medium). We will always do our best to attend to you as quickly as possible.

Can I access Carbon from any location?

Carbon is currently available throughout Nigeria.

Loans

How do I apply for a loan?

After downloading the app, sign in or sign up to identify yourself and this will take you to our brief registration/application forms. Complete an application with valid information, then determine how much you would like to borrow. We will then display your available loan offer in the app and you can view your expected repayment(s). Submit your application and we will typically notify you of the loan decision within minutes.

How long does it take to process a Carbon loan?

The entire process can be completed in no time! Once you complete our short application and accept the loan offer provided, your information is reviewed, and a loan decision is typically provided within minutes. If approved, funds will be received within a few moments of the application process being completed.

How much can I borrow from Carbon? For how long?

When you fill out an application, our system will calculate a loan offer for you which determines the amount and repayment duration we can offer you. This differs by individual, so please ensure your information is accurate and that you are applying from your personal mobile device, for us to provide you with the best possible loan offer.

My current loan offer is too small! Can I get a higher loan amount?

The loan offer displayed to you is what we can provide at the time of application. This may vary in future - as we learn more about you, we’ll also be able to provide loans that better suit your financial profile.

I was approved for a loan, why have I not yet received my funds?

If there is currently no debit card linked to your account, your debit/ATM card needs to be setup in order for your funds to be disbursed to your account. Please click 'Setup Debit/ATM Card' in the app and add a card to be used for repayment. For your convenience, we’ll deduct your repayment automatically on your due date.
If there is a card linked to your account, please ensure it expires a minimum of one month after your final loan repayment date for it to be accepted as valid for funds disbursement.
Please note: you may need to have at least NGN100 in the account linked to the card for setup to be successful. Card setup is only required once, as long as your card is valid.

My loan status shows ‘Disbursed’, but I am yet to receive funds.

Funds are typically received instantly after receiving disbursement confirmation. Just check in your Carbon Wallet for your cash.

Why was my loan application rejected?

There are a few reasons behind loan application rejections. The most likely reason is that our system did not receive sufficient data to score your application. Please ensure you are signed into Carbon using your main mobile device, and also keep your data on while using the app in order for us to determine the best possible loan offer for you.
Another possible reason is that you may not have shared all of the information required in the application or provided invalid details.
However, there can be other reasons. As a responsible lender, we take steps to ensure we only extend credit to applicants who can afford it, so that our clients won’t become too indebted. It is possible that at this time, you don’t match the profile of individual we normally lend money to.
Negative repayment patterns on previous obligations (including Carbon loans) can also limit access to credit, or make it more expensive to obtain.
Whatever the situation, we recommend keeping your app installed and using it to perform other transactions, as this boosts your eligibility for loans.

Does my account need to be funded before I apply for a loan?

Our loans are intended to meet your urgent cash needs, so we understand you may be low on funds at the time of application. However, on your first loan, we process a one-time card setup charge which can be up to NGN100 when your loan is approved. This is done to verify that the debit/ATM card your repayment will be charged from is valid.

I have submitted my application but I have not yet received any notification on my loan status.

Notifications are typically sent via SMS and/or email immediately after your loan application is sent. If you have not received either of these, please check the ‘Your Loans’ menu in the app to confirm the current status of your request.

My loan status is showing as ‘Pending’ after I have submitted my application. How do I proceed?

This status indicates that our system is processing your application. You will receive a notification on your loan decision shortly after.

I’m receiving an error: “Location not found. Unable to submit loan application”. How do I resolve this?

Please ensure that location settings are activated on your Android device, in order for the application to submit successfully. After this is done, please exit the Carbon app, then re-open and attempt to submit your application again. We also recommend that you ensure you are in an area with stable network connection while the app detects your location – sometimes, even taking your device outdoors briefly can help, as network signal is typically stronger.

Savings & Investments

What’s PayVest?

PayVest is Carbon’s investment platform that helps you put away funds and grow your wealth in a secure and transparent manner.

How much interest do I get on my PayVest investment?

The prevailing interest rate for a 1-year investment is 15.5%* per annum. Rates will vary depending on the investment duration selected.
*Note: Withholding tax is applied on interest earned. Rates may be subject to change over time.

How much can I invest?

On each investment plan, qualifying investment amounts range from ₦50,000 to ₦10,000,000.

How long can I invest for?

Currently, available investment tenors are 3, 6, 9 or 12 months. If you wish to re-invest after an investment has been paid out, it just takes a few clicks in your app.

How is interest calculated?

Interest rates are calculated based on the investment tenor selected. The longer you lock in funds for, the higher your rates!

Can I have more than one investment plan?

Yes! At any point in time, you can create up to 5 (five) investment plans in your Carbon app.
If you need to have multiple investments towards various goals (for example -  a short 3-month investment towards a vacation you’re planning, or setting up a 12-month retirement fund at a higher interest rate than your pension) then Payvest is just right for you.

How can I fund my Payvest investment account?

You can lock in your Payvest investment by doing the following:

  1. Click ‘Activate Payvest’ on the home page of your app. You’ll need to have your app updated to access the new feature.
  2. Select your investment terms and confirm the investment.
  3. We’ll display a customized bank account for you to transfer the investment amount to.
  4. Once done, your investment is instantly confirmed! 💰

Can I top up an existing investment?

No. However, if you wish to make additional investments, you can setup another investment plan and lock in the same rates

Is there a fee to setup a PayVest investment?

Not at all. It is absolutely FREE to opt in and earn guaranteed interest on your investment, once you lock in funds.

What is a maturity date?

This is the beautiful day when your investment has completed the agreed time period, and you receive your full principal and interest payout to your account! 😃

How can I make an early repayment?

If you want to make a payment prior to your due date, please use the ’Make a Repayment’ button on the 'Your Loans' page on the app.

If I make an early repayment, will I pay less?

Early repayments are accepted and you can apply for a new loan immediately your previous loan is paid off.
However, please note that an early repayment does not imply that the terms of your loan obligation will change. Your repayment terms are fixed at the point of loan application and the total amount to be paid is not changed by an early repayment.

I just paid back my most recent Carbon loan, but I see a waiting period in my app before I can reapply. Why is that?

We have system limits in place to avoid excessive usage of the platform, which can be financially damaging to our customers.
You can submit a maximum of 2 loan applications every 15 days, and receive a maximum of 2 Carbon loans every 30 days. When reached, a waiting period is displayed.

Can I reschedule my loan repayment date?

Please note that due dates are fixed when your loan is approved. We do not currently reschedule due dates, so please ensure that you make your repayment on/before your agreed due date in order to continue having this service available to you.

What happens if I can’t make a payment on my due date?

We understand that financial circumstances can change suddenly, however, it is important to note that we do not schedule repayment dates and you should only take a Carbon loan if you are reasonably certain you have the means to repay within the agreed period.
There may be fees associated with late repayments. In addition, defaulting loans are reported to national Credit Bureaus and this can severely affect your ability to access loans in future. We therefore advise you take every effort to meet your obligations on time.

What happens if I don't pay back my loan?

We want to keep you as a customer, so please make every attempt to pay us back on time. If you find yourself in a situation that makes it difficult to make your scheduled repayment, please contact us immediately by sending an email to customer@getcarbon.co or calling 01-460 9945.
We do our best to help our customers avoid these situations and the resulting consequences which can include:
Suspension of your access to our lending service
Reporting your account delinquency to multiple credit bureaus

Other forms of repayment can lead to delays with account updates.   9. Name of ATM branch funds were transferred from (if applicable)

Please note: Repayments made via cash transfer will be updated on our platform within 1 - 2 business days of receiving your email.

I just paid back my most recent Carbon loan. How soon can I apply for another loan?

You can check your current status by logging into your account. If you have successfully paid off your previous loan on time, you can typically re-apply for another loan shortly after and may qualify for higher credit at a lower rate.

Security & Privacy

Can I trust Carbon with my data?

When using Carbon, your data is always secure. Our platform uses world-class standard security and encryption technology to protect all details. When you provide us with information, you can also be assured of your privacy – our loan agreements are always kept confidential.

Who has access to my data?

Carbon never shares your personal details with third parties, unless it is for dedicated business purposes, such as reporting of loan defaulters to authorized Credit Bureaus. It is therefore important to ensure that you repay your loan on time so your credit history is not negatively affected. If you make timely repayments, this increases your chances of accessing credit from financial institutions in future.

What is a credit bureau?

Credit bureaus are agencies that provide information on individuals’ borrowing habits and financial patterns. They typically work with financial institutions and other creditors to collate this data, and your performance on previous reported accounts impact your ability to obtain loans or other financial services in future. The more loans you repay on time, the better your credit history gets. With this, you’ll be able to access better loans at cheaper rates.

How can I review my data that Carbon sends to credit bureaus?

You can now view your credit report directly in your Carbon app! Select the Credit Report menu in the app to view your credit history from any of the 3 nationally-registered credit bureaus. You may also contact the bureaus directly, in the event you have any issues with the information contained on your report:

(www.firstcentralcreditbureau.com )

(www.crservicesplc.com) or

Why do you need permission to my mobile device?

Carbon uses data from your mobile device, including (but not limited to) location, financial SMS, contacts and notifications to provide you with the best possible loan offer. It’s an important part of our evaluation process and allows us to provide you with loans and personal finance management most suitable for you.
You are completely in control of the permissions you grant us, and can revoke this at any time you wish.

How do I give Carbon permission for my phone?

On your mobile device, kindly go to Settings -> Apps -> Carbon -> Permissions and ensure you have provided Carbon with access to the requested permissions.

What happens if I lose my device?

You're safe! Your data provided in the app is secured by a 4 digit PIN created by and only accessible to you. This PIN is requested each time you open the app. Once you get a new device, just login to your account and your data will be right where you left it.

I have forgotten my Carbon PIN. How can I get it back?

If you do not remember your PIN, launch the app, sign in and select 'forgot PIN'. Enter the requested personal details in order to verify yourself and reset your PIN.

I did not receive my phone verification code, what do I do?

When you sign up on our service, you should receive an SMS from us to the mobile number you provided. However there are situations which might delay SMS receipt.


  1. If your line has ever been ported from one network to another, please reach out to us via our support channels for further assistance.
  2. If you are certain that you did not receive a message, please ensure that your provided number is switched on and is in an area with good network service to receive the SMS. You should also check with your mobile network provider to confirm that your line is enabled to receive such messages from 3rd party services.

Note: A data connection is required in order to validate your phone number.

Referrals & Rewards

What is a referral link?

A referral code is a unique link assigned to you, which you can then use to share our service with friends. If anyone signs up for Carbon using your code, you become eligible for benefits, such as loan discounts, when a referral repays a loan on time.
You can share your referral link by clicking the ‘Refer a Friend’ menu in your app.

I’ve introduced my friend to Carbon and I want to apply for a loan on his/her behalf. Am I allowed to do this?

3rd party applications are not accepted on Carbon. Please ensure you do not complete an application for someone else or login on your device with another individual’s profile, as this can suspend your access to our service. Encourage friends to apply for a Carbon loan themselves and on their own devices.

How do I earn loan discounts?

Carbon offers a discount program that allows you to earn money for referring a friend - who then repays their first loan on time - to our service. When you bring in a good borrower to help grow the community, we’ll reward by offering you a discount on your next loan. Just click the ‘Use Discount’ button that becomes available on the Loan Details page and apply it before submitting an application.

How many discounts can I apply on a single loan?

You can only apply one discount per loan.

I’ve referred a friend, but did not earn a discount on my next loan – what happened?

Discounts have a validity period after which they expire, if it remains unused. Please ensure you apply your discount to your loans before it expires. For more information on discounts, please contact us via our support channels.

Google Play Store

I previously used Carbon’s web platform, but I’m now redirected to the Google Play Store to download the mobile app. Why?

In order to provide a better mobile experience for you, certain features on Carbon may be app-only. This means that users of our web platform will need to migrate to the Android app in order to continue enjoying the particular service.

I’m trying to download the mobile app from the Play Store, but I’m getting the following error: “This Item Isn’t Available in Your Country”. How do I fix this?

This means that your Google Play account is currently set in a location where the app is available.

I’m trying to download the app but it is not installing on my device. Help!

Carbon currently only supports Android devices running on version 4.1 and above. Please check your device settings to verify whether your device meets this requirement.

Google Play Store

What is Carbon Wallet?

This is a fully-functional e-wallet which allows you to store value on Carbon, perform all your financial transactions and earn discounts and rewards for use.

Are there any charges or fees associated with using my Carbon Wallet?

Absolutely not! With Carbon Wallet, your money remains all yours. There are no associated charges for having funds in your wallet.

What are my transaction limits on Wallet?

If you have gone through all our KYC verification checks, you will be able to perform transactions of up to ₦1m daily on Carbon.

How do I transfer money from my wallet to a different account?

To transfer money from your wallet, simply click the ‘Withdraw’ button on the Wallet section of your app.

How do I fund my wallet?

Carbon currently only supports Android devices running on version 4.1 and above. Please check your device settings to verify whether your device meets this requirement.

Are my funds safe in the wallet?

Yes. We’ve partnered with a licensed MFB to offer this wallet, which means that all deposits are NDIC-insured. This demonstrates that whenever you use Carbon Wallet, your protection is guaranteed. 🔐

Do I earn interest in my wallet?

Funds stored in your wallet do not earn interest. If you’re interested in earning interest on your money, we recommend trying out our Payvest investment feature in the app.

What’s the difference between my wallet and Payvest?

Great question! While both the wallet and Payvest plans can store value for you, the key difference is that Payvest allows you to set aside money to invest and earn higher interest rates on these investments, while your wallet is mainly used to perform transactions.

Can I repay my loan from my wallet?

Most certainly! You can perform all transactions available on Carbon with your wallet. This also includes using funds in your wallet to repay a loan.
